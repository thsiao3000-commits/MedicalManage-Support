中文版

「家庭藥箱」（下稱「本 App」）尊重並重視您的隱私。本政策說明我們如何處理您在使用本 App 時所建立的資料。

1. 我們收集什麼

您在使用本 App 時可能輸入或產生以下類型的資料：

  • 健康相關資料：藥品名稱、用法、提醒時間、服藥紀錄、漏吃藥紀錄、過敏與病史、血壓、血糖、體重、體脂率、身高
  • 使用者內容：藥品照片、藥品備註、家人個人檔案（姓名、暱稱、頭像、角色）

2. 資料儲存於何處

  • 所有資料儲存於您本人的 iCloud 帳號（透過 Apple 的 CloudKit 服務）。本 App 開發者並未架設任何伺服器收集或保存您的資料。
  • 您的資料以加密方式由 Apple 託管於您的私人 iCloud 資料庫，僅您的 Apple ID 可存取。
  • 透過 iCloud 多裝置同步機制，您可以在登入相同 Apple ID 的其他裝置上看到自己的資料。

3. 與家人共享（代管模式）

  • 本 App 提供「遠端代管」功能，讓您主動透過 Apple CKShare 機制邀請家人查看您指定的長輩資料。
  • 共享是雙方自願且可隨時撤回（owner 端可停止代管、recipient 端可離開分享）。
  • 共享內容仍透過 Apple iCloud 在被邀請者之間傳遞，不經本 App 開發者的伺服器。

4. 選用 AI 功能（自備 API Key）

  • 本 App 提供藥品包裝拍照辨識、藥品交互作用分析等選用功能，需要您自行向 OpenAI 或 Groq 申請 API Key 並填入設定頁。
  • 啟用這些功能時，您的藥品照片或藥品名稱與用途會直接從您的裝置傳送至您指定的 AI 服務商（OpenAI 或 Groq），開發者不接觸這些資料。
  • 上述資料的使用受 OpenAI 或 Groq 自身的隱私政策與服務條款規範。
  • 不使用 AI 功能完全不影響本 App 主要功能。

5. Apple Health（HealthKit）整合

  • 本 App 在您授權後可從 Apple Health 讀取血糖、血壓、體重、體脂率資料，整合至健康追蹤頁面。
  • 採唯讀方式 — 本 App 不會寫入任何資料回 Apple Health。
  • 您可隨時於 iOS「設定 > 隱私權與安全性 > 健康」中撤銷授權。

6. 我們不做的事

  • 不向第三方銷售或分享您的資料
  • 不接入第三方分析、廣告、追蹤工具（如 Google Analytics、Firebase、Crashlytics、Meta SDK 等皆未使用）
  • 不收集裝置 ID、廣告識別碼或位置資訊
  • 不在 App 內顯示廣告

7. 您的權利

  • 匯出：可於 App 內以 .medbox 格式匯出您的全部資料
  • 刪除：可於 App 內刪除任何單筆紀錄、家人或藥品；亦可於 iOS「設定 > Apple ID > iCloud > 管理儲存空間」整批刪除本 App 的 iCloud 資料
  • 撤銷分享：可於 App 內隨時撤回您發出或收到的代管邀請

8. 兒童隱私

本 App 並非針對 13 歲以下兒童設計，亦不會主動向兒童收集資料。

9. 政策更新

本政策若有更新，將於本頁面公布並更新生效日期。重大變更將以 App 內公告通知。

10. 聯絡方式

如對本政策有任何疑問，請聯絡：thsiao4000@gmail.com

English

This Privacy Policy describes how 家庭藥箱 ("the App") handles the information you create when using it.

1. What we collect

While using the App, you may enter or generate the following types of data:

  • Health-related data: medication names, dosages, reminder schedules, dosage records, missed-dose records, allergies, medical history, blood pressure, blood glucose, weight, body fat percentage, height
  • User content: medication photos, notes, family member profiles (name, nickname, avatar, role)

2. Where data is stored

  • All data is stored in your own iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. The App developer does not operate any server that collects or retains your data.
  • Your data is encrypted and held by Apple in your private iCloud database, accessible only to your Apple ID.
  • Through iCloud multi-device sync, you can access your data on other devices signed in to the same Apple ID.

3. Sharing with family (Caregiver mode)

  • The App provides a "Remote Caregiver" feature that lets you voluntarily invite family members via Apple's CKShare mechanism to view data for specific elders you designate.
  • Sharing is mutually voluntary and revocable at any time (the owner can stop sharing; the recipient can leave a share).
  • Shared content is still transmitted via Apple iCloud between participants and does not pass through the developer's servers.

4. Optional AI features (Bring-Your-Own API Key)

  • The App offers optional features for medication photo recognition and drug-interaction analysis. These require you to obtain your own API key from OpenAI or Groq and enter it in Settings.
  • When you use these features, your medication photo or medication name and purpose are transmitted directly from your device to the AI provider (OpenAI or Groq) you selected. The developer does not see this data.
  • Use of such data is governed by OpenAI's or Groq's own privacy policies and terms of service.
  • Choosing not to use AI features has no effect on the App's primary functionality.

5. Apple Health (HealthKit) integration

  • With your authorization, the App can read blood glucose, blood pressure, weight, and body fat percentage data from Apple Health to integrate into the health tracking page.
  • Access is read-only — the App does not write data back to Apple Health.
  • You can revoke authorization at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

6. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDK (no Google Analytics, Firebase, Crashlytics, Meta SDK, etc.)
  • We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data
  • We do not display advertisements within the App

7. Your rights

  • Export: You can export all your data as a .medbox file from within the App
  • Deletion: You can delete any individual record, family member, or medication within the App; you can also bulk-delete this App's iCloud data via iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage
  • Revoke sharing: You can revoke any caregiver share you have sent or received at any time within the App

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Policy updates

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Material changes will be announced within the App.

10. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: thsiao4000@gmail.com
